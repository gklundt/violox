The proprietary mobile alert system (MAS) by Violox is an application that we have developed to take away the annoying need for a property owner, manager, or agent to ever have to pick up a phone and call a tow company. How it works: Once a parking violation is located, the agent inputs basic vehicle information into MAS, clicks submit and that information is submitted to any local parking enforcement agent in your area that is authorized to enforce on your property.
